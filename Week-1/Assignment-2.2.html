About

ISKCON JUHU TEMPLE

Their kirtan is truly enchanting, an experience that beckons everyone to join at least once. The harmonious melodies and soul-stirring chants create an ambiance that is both soothing and uplifting. It's an invitation to immerse oneself in the sacred sounds and connect with a higher spiritual realm.

Temple Prasadam

The temple prasadam is an absolute delight, a culinary experience that tantalizes the taste buds and leaves an indelible impression on the senses. With every bite, one is transported to a realm of exquisite flavors and textures that are nothing short of a gastronomic masterpiece.

Bhagavad gita class

The lecture on the Bhagavad Gita is truly awe-inspiring. It is a profound discourse that illuminates the essence of life, spirituality, and the human journey. The wisdom imparted in the lecture resonates deeply, offering profound insights that have the power to transform one's perspective and guide them on a path of self-discovery.

Things To Do

  • Visit the temple on sunday once. Take the darshan of lord shri shri radherasbhariji.
  • Attend the Bhagavad Gita class.
  • Take prasadam and assoicate with temple devotees.
